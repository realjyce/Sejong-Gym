Class Protocols

Train In High-Energy Cycles Led By Technical Coaches

Every class is built around movement quality first, then intensity. Whether you are new or advanced, our coaches scale each session so you can push hard and still recover properly.

Pulse HIIT

Tue & Thu: 7:00 PM - 8:00 PM

Fast interval rounds, metabolic finishers, and monitored intensity zones.

Modern Yoga Recovery

Fri: 5:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Mobility, breathing control, and postural reset for better weekly recovery.

Personal Engineering
